SQL MURDER

Investigating the murder in SQL City on January 15th, 2018, this project started with the search for the crime scene report, showing SQL City as the location and the specific date. This led to the identification of the witnesses. The first witness is located through the last house on Northwestern Dr, while the second witness, Annabel, resides on Franklin Ave. Following the witness identification, an interview with the witnesses provided information needed to identify the suspect.
